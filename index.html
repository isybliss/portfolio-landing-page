CGPA
Calculator

Calculating Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) can be hard. That’s why we made CGPA Calculator — a user friendly app for students to manage their academic information and track their academic progress

Try for free

User Registration and Login

Users can create an account by providing their name, email address and password. The email address and password provided is used to log into the CGPA calculator

Dashboard

Users are presented with a dashboard that displays their personal information, academic details and navigation links to different sections

Course Management

Users can add, edit and delte courses. Each course entry include information such as course code, course title, course unit and grade. These details are used to calculate the Grade Point Average
